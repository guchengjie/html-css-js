日期过滤器

日期的默认格式：{{now}}

date过滤器的shortDate：{{now|date:'shortDate'}}

date过滤器的mediumDate：{{now|date:'mediumDate'}}

date过滤器的longDate：{{now|date:'longDate'}}

date过滤器的fullDate：{{now|date:'fullDate'}}

date过滤器的shortTime：{{now|date:'shortTime'}}

date过滤器的mediumTime：{{now|date:'mediumTime'}}

date过滤器的short：{{now|date:'short'}}

date过滤器的medium：{{now|date:'medium'}}

date过滤器的自定义格式：{{now|date:'y年MM月d日 H:m:ss sss a EEEE'}}


lowercase和uppercase

lowercase过滤器：{{"Hello World"|lowercase}}

uppercase过滤器：{{"Hello World"|uppercase}}
